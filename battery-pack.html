Battery Pack Design for Solar EV

UBC Solar — Jan 2025 to Mar 2025

Designed and developed a 32S13P Li-ion battery pack delivering 5.25 kWh @ 120 V. Optimized for safety, modularity, and manufacturability through CFD-based thermal modeling.

Key Highlights

  • 7–8 mΩ contact resistance using non-welded design
  • CAD optimized for compact 25×20×8 inch housing
  • Validated cooling airflow via Ansys & Altair CFD (+15% improvement)
  • Automated OCV + impedance testing in Python
  • Custom PCBs in Altium & 3D printed fixtures

Tools Used

SolidWorks | Altium Designer | Python | CFD | Composite Fabrication
