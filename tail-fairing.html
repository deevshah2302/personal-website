Carbon Fibre Tail Fairing

UBC Thunderbikes - Jan 2024 to March 2024

Designed the motorcycle tail fairing and gas tank assembly to enhance aerodynamic form, ergonomics, and manufacturability. Utilized photogrammetry and CNC machining to ensure accurate geometry capture and precise mold fabrication.

Key Highlights

  • Used photogrammetry to generate a detailed 3D model of the motorcycle gas tank from high-resolution images.
  • Processed point clouds and surface meshes into CAD geometry for design alignment and reference.
  • Designed and CNC-machined foam molds for the tail fairing and gas tank, ensuring dimensional accuracy and smooth composite layup surfaces.
  • Manufactured gas tank and tail fairing using vacuum-assisted wet layup techniques

Tools Used

Wet Layups | Photogrammetry | SolidWorks | CNC
