Battery Pack Design for Solar EV

UBC Solar — Jan 2025 – Mar 2025

Designed and developed a 32S13P Li-ion battery pack for UBC Solar’s race vehicle, delivering 5.25 kWh @ 120 V. Optimized for safety, serviceability, and manufacturability using CFD-based thermal modeling.

Highlights

  • 7–8 mΩ non-welded contact resistance.
  • Validated airflow via Ansys & Altair CFD (15 % improvement).
  • OCV + impedance testing automation in Python for 700+ cells.
  • Custom Altium PCB design + 3D-printed fixtures.
